## 2. The Black Box Autopsy: Model Behaviors & Lessons

### A. Turnaround Operational Leverage (Major Success)
*   **Key Learn:** Turnaround assets with high operational leverage and fixed-cost bases (like engine servicing facilities) are the ultimate wealth creators when priced at depressed levels (Jan 2023 P/E < 10x equivalent on normalized cash flow). As widebody engine flying hours recovered, cash flows turned net-positive, triggering massive, risk-free equity re-ratings.

### B. The "Trailing Stop Whipsaw" Risk (Near Miss / Drag)
*   **Incident:** In March 2023, December 2023, and March 2025, the model executed partial exits (selling 50%) due to RSI overbought levels. Because the turnaround trend was structurally strong and backed by structural cash flows, consolidations were brief, and the stock resumed its climb. This resulted in minor **re-entry drag** (buying back at slightly higher prices or missing short-term gains).
*   **Mitigation:** For secular turnaround plays with multi-year cash flow expansion targets, relax the RSI exit threshold or use a **longer-term moving average (e.g., 50-day EMA)** as the trailing stop, rather than a short-term 20-day line, to prevent premature exits on structural winners.
