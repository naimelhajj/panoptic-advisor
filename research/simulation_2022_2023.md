# June 2022 – June 2023 Active Portfolio Simulation (The Income-Target Fallacy)

This document details an active portfolio simulation from **June 1, 2022, to June 1, 2023**, starting with **$300** capital. The simulation evaluates two goals:
1.  Reach **>$5,000** by November 1, 2022.
2.  Generate a **steady minimum of $1,500/month** in income thereafter.

The simulation is conducted sequentially, simulating real-time decisions on the exact trading days **without hindsight**, strictly applying the **First-Principles Bottleneck & Valuation (FPBV)** rules.

## 2. Autopsy: The Required Income Trap

This simulation demonstrates the mathematical and psychological trap of **Required Target Returns**:

1.  **Arbitrary Milestones vs. Market Realities:**
    *   The market operates in cycles and only offers high-probability setups when technical extremes (RSI < 30) or clear catalysts align.
    *   Imposing an arbitrary monthly income target ($1,500/month on $5,000) forces a trader to manufacture trades in environments with no edge.
2.  **The Option Decay Trap:**
    *   When forced to generate fast returns, traders inevitably turn to leveraged options. Since options suffer from time decay (theta) and require precise timing, consecutive forced option plays have a near-100% probability of expiring worthless during consolidation phases.
3.  **Capital Erosion:**
    *   Withdrawing capital during drawdown months permanently reduces the trading base, making it mathematically impossible to recover.
