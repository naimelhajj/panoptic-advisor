## 3. Black Box Autopsy: Lessons from Active Re-allocation

1.  **Compound Drag of Transaction Costs:** 
    *   In a small account ($200), each $1.00 commission represents a significant percentage of the trade value (e.g., buying 1 AMD for $13 with a $1.00 fee is a **7.3% transaction cost**). 
    *   *Mitigation:* Active re-allocations should only be executed in batch blocks when cash values are high enough to dilute commission drag to <0.5% per trade.
2.  **Oversold Arbitrage in Secular Winners:**
    *   Buying high-moat bottleneck stocks at extreme oversold RSI levels (RSI < 25) consistently yielded rapid 30%–50% recoveries within 1–2 months (e.g., AMD in Dec 2020, Shopify in Nov 2022, Rolls-Royce in Dec 2023). This is the highest probability short-term trading setup in the market.
