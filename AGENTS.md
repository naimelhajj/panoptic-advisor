# Agent Instructions

This project uses `project-memory/` as the source of truth for cross-chat continuity.

Read before working:
- [project-purpose.md](file:///C:/development/stocks-finder/project-memory/project-purpose.md)
- [project-progress.md](file:///C:/development/stocks-finder/project-memory/project-progress.md)
- [project-history.md](file:///C:/development/stocks-finder/project-memory/project-history.md)
- [project-black-box.md](file:///C:/development/stocks-finder/project-memory/project-black-box.md)

Update after meaningful changes:
- Append to `project-history.md` for every notable change, decision, or handoff.
- Update `project-progress.md` when the percent, milestones, blockers, or next step change.
- Refine `project-purpose.md` when the goal is clarified, expanded, or narrowed.
- Append to `project-black-box.md` whenever something fails, surprises you, regresses, or almost fails.

Rules:
- Keep history and black-box append-only.
- Keep purpose as the current goal plus a refinement log.
- Keep progress grounded in completed acceptance criteria, not effort spent.
- Record concrete dates and brief facts.
- Do not rely on chat history alone; treat these files as the durable memory.
